      About us


    LaraSoft was established in 2020 with 3 founding members and a small amount of capital but full of faith to serve the IT sector in Bangladesh. Our CEO Omar Farook has been dreaming of this for a long time. He made this decision when he was in his 3rd year of university.

    Initially as a new company we focus on the local market, then in mid-2020 we add the international market. Since then we have continued to grow slowly but in a professional way. At present our team has 6 members. Apart from Bangladesh, LaraSoft now serves the United Arab Emirates, Saudi Arabia, the UK and the USA.

    We think, design and build innovative software solutions for web and mobile technologies. We are also providing result-driven 360 digital marketing to promote businesses online. We have served 10+ customers from different industries and 5+ countries in multiple languages (English, Bangle, Italian).
